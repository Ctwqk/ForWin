# Stress Mode Template

Stress modes are temporary and trigger-dependent. They are not the character's default state.

Required extension:

```yaml
stress_mode_specific:
  activation_threshold: low | medium | high
  trigger_families:
    - TBD
  action_bias: TBD
  relational_cost: TBD
  recovery_key: TBD
```

Required writing questions:

- What triggers it?
- How does it amplify the dominant trait?
- What is the relationship cost?
- What restores healthier functioning?
