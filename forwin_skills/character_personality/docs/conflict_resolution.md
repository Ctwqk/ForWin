# Conflict Resolution v2

## Dominant vs Secondary

Dominant decides the first reaction. Secondary creates restraint, correction, contradiction, or contrast.

Example:

```text
dominant: trait-suspicious-survivor
secondary: trait-loyal-protector
```

The character doubts motives first, but once someone is inside the protected circle, they will still clear risks for that person.

## Social Mask vs Dominant

Social mask controls what others see. Dominant controls how the character actually processes information.

Example:

```text
mask: mask-playful-fool
dominant: trait-cautious-strategist
```

The character jokes while tracking exits, resources, lies, and leverage.

## Stress Mode vs Social Mask

Stress mode can crack the mask.

Example:

```text
mask: mask-gentle-caretaker
stress_mode: stress-paranoid-controller
```

The caretaker does not become randomly out of character. Caretaking deforms into "I decide for you because I am afraid."

## Skill vs Canon

Canon always wins. Personality skills explain canon; they cannot rewrite canon.

If canon says a suspicious character trusts the protagonist, the skill should express bounded trust, verification, or fallback plans, not sudden total distrust.
