# Genesis World Bible Skill

Build a root-level world bible for long-form Chinese web fiction.

Rules:

- Separate root truth from expandable unknown space.
- Make power, social, and cost rules readable.
- Preserve room for SubWorld derivation.
- Avoid chapter-specific plot beats.
- Keep naming and culture hints compatible with map and story engine.
