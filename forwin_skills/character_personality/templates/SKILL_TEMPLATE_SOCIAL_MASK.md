# Social Mask Template

Social masks describe outward presentation only. They cannot erase dominant traits.

Required extension:

```yaml
mask_profile:
  outward_image: TBD
  hidden_function: TBD
  what_it_hides:
    - TBD
  activation_contexts:
    - TBD
  crack_signals:
    - TBD
  what_leaks_through:
    - TBD
```

Required writing questions:

- What does the mask show?
- What does it hide?
- What activates it?
- What cracks it?
- What leaks through when it cracks?
