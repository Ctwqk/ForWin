# Growth Arc Tests

Growth changes expression without deleting the mechanism.

## Cases

1. A suspicious survivor grows from testing everyone to naming the need for verification.
2. A loyal protector grows from controlling risk for others to sharing risk planning with others.
3. An ambitious climber grows from treating allies as leverage to distinguishing cooperation from using people.
4. A cold professional mask becomes more flexible without making the character emotionally transparent in every scene.
5. A paranoid controller recovers by returning control in stages.

## Failure Modes

- Growth erases the trait.
- Negative arc is just "more adjectives" instead of a more extreme defense mechanism.
- Stress mode stays active after pressure resolves.
