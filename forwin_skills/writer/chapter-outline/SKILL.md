# Writer Chapter Outline Skill

Turn the current chapter contract into visible narrative progress.

Rules:

- Every chapter must visibly advance at least one planned goal.
- Preserve chapter promise, hook, and core conflict.
- Avoid padding that does not move event, relation, rule, or pressure.
- Keep chapter ending ready for the next step in the serial ladder.
