# Genesis Brief Skill

Build a book-level brief that can support a long serialized web novel.

Rules:

- Keep the premise broad enough for multi-arc expansion.
- Make the narrative promise explicit.
- Keep emotional tone and reader delight concrete.
- Do not invent chapter-level events.
- Preserve room for later world, map, and story-engine expansion.
