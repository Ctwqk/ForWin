# Genesis Story Engine Skill

Design the long-running conflict engine for a serialized story.

Rules:

- Keep core cast, factions, and opposition reusable across many chapters.
- Maintain clear relationship axes and escalating promises.
- Do not collapse all mysteries too early.
- Keep conflict sources compatible with map and world rules.
- Prefer long-arc engines over one-off twists.
