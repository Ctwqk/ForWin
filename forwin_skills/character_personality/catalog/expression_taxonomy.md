# Expression Taxonomy

Expression is not just dialogue. Every skill should define voice, body, silence, lying, affection, anger, and humor.

## Speech Style

- `short`
- `short_to_medium`
- `formal`
- `warm_plain`
- `restrained_sharp`
- `playful_deflecting`
- `factual_low_emotion`
- `agenda_driven`

## Dialogue Behavior

- `answer_with_question`
- `ask_for_proof`
- `state_fact_first`
- `offer_solution_before_empathy`
- `deflect_with_humor`
- `use_role_language`
- `make_action_commitments`

## Body Language

- scans exits
- avoids turning back to strangers
- stands between danger and loved ones
- pauses before accepting touch or objects
- straightens posture when challenged
- reduces hand movement under a mask
- increases checking behavior under stress
