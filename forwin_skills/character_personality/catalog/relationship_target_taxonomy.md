# Relationship Target Taxonomy

Relationship behavior must vary by target.

## Required Targets

- `stranger`
- `ally`
- `loved_one`
- `enemy`

## Recommended Targets

- `superior`
- `subordinate`
- `rival`
- `betrayer`
- `old_friend`
- `mentor`
- `dependent`

## Runtime Boundary

Relationship pattern skills do not store live scores such as trust, resentment, debt, attraction, or fear. Those come from scene relationship state and are merged at runtime.
