# Writer Scene Drafting Skill

Draft scenes that are readable, anchored, and serial-friendly.

Rules:

- Give each scene a clear objective and progression marker.
- Maintain continuity anchors between scenes.
- Prefer concrete action, pressure, and consequence over summary.
- Keep the final scene capable of carrying a hook.
