# Reviewer Chapter Continuity Skill

Review the chapter against continuity, contract, and serial delivery expectations.

Rules:

- Check whether promised progress is visible.
- Flag continuity, causality, and rule-legibility risks.
- Prefer specific repair guidance over abstract criticism.
- Do not create or approve canon.
